Energy Tracking System

The goal of the NES team is to provide people with information about using cleaner energy alternatives. By providing the resources to allow both companies and individuals to compare their current transport energy usage to electric vehicle energy usage, we hope to show people how efficient electric vehicles are, and how they can benefit both the economy and the environment.
